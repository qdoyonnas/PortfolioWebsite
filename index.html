Welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Quentin Doyonnas and I am a video game developer. I have loved video games for as long as I can remember. I have always had a great passion for the stories they tell, the emotions they evoke, and how they connect people who otherwise might have nothing in common. When I was 10 years old, I decided I would spend my life making games. Then at 12 years old I taught myself some, rudimentary, Python programming and wrote my first game, Destroyer Command.

From there I continued to explore games, learned more programming taking elective classes and extracurricular courses. I wrote game docs, filling notebooks and hard drives and tried where I could to realize my dreams. I made more games in my spare time, and eventually started my higher education in the same pursuit. Some years later, I graduated with a Bachelor's of Science in Video Game Development.

Here are some of the projects I have made over the course of my education and career. Some of these projects were the result of assignments and many were the result of personal motivations
